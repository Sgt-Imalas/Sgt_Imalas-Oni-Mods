[h1]Introduction[/h1]

This mod allows to change the output direction of the radbolt chamber.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] Selectable output direction on the radbolt chamber
[*] Custom anim for the different directions
[*] Mod can be safely removed, all radbolt chambers will revert to facing upwards

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Known Issues[/h1]
[list]
[*] the output tile indicator is shiftet to occupy the same tile as the input, this is purely visual
[/list]
