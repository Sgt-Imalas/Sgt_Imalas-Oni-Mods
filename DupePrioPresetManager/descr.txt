[h1]Preset Manager[/h1]

This mod adds presets to consumables, priorities and schedules.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Consumable Presets: store and load presets about what duplicants are allowed or forbidden to consume
[/list][img]https://i.imgur.com/suuJyFf.gif[/img][list]
[*]Priority Presets: store and load presets for duplicant task priorities
[/list][img]https://i.imgur.com/ucFq0Hc.gif[/img][list]
[*]Schedule Presets: store and load presets for schedules
[/list][img]https://i.imgur.com/R0LCYEx.gif[/img][list]
[*]Default Schedules: Mark schedule presets as "default" - you then can automatically create schedules for each preset marked like that with one click (ideal for new games)
[/list][img]https://i.imgur.com/ltnWF4V.gif[/img]

[h1]Want more Presets?[/h1]
[list][*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2856555858]DSS - Duplicant Presets:[/url] store and load attribute & trait presets for printing pod and starter duplicants. skins are separatly selectable.
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2947429297]CGM - Custom Cluster Presets:[/url] Create your own clusters and save them as presets to either do a new playthrough later or to share the custom cluster with friends.
[/list]
[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
The presets should be inherently compatible with other mods that add new consumables, priorities, schedule blocks, 
Tested compatibilities:
[list][*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2786087434]Engineer Build Priority[/url]
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2373109589]Rest for the Weary[/url]
[/list]
