[h1]Amphibious Minnow (Species Mod)[/h1]

This mod turns Minnow into a new, fully amphibious duplicant species that has gills and can breathe any water, along with some brand new adaptive skills.

Requires aquatic planet pack!
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Sgt-Imalas/Sgt_Imalas-Oni-Mods/refs/heads/master/Compat_All_DLC5_Required.png[/img]

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] new duplicant species: "Amphibious Duplicant"
[*] Minnow is now an amphibious duplicant when unlocked from the story trait (does not(!) affect already spawned Minnows)
[*] Amphibious duplicants come with innate swimming skills and increased resistence to liquid based afflictions
[*] Amphibious duplicants can breathe both regular air as well as filter oxygen from any waters (polluted water and brine will trigger itchy gills in the beginning)
[*] Amphibious duplicants cannot access regular swimming traits (due to them being innate)
[*] Amphibious duplicants gain access to 4 brand new "Adaptation" skills that allow them to adapt better to their environments
[*] Amphibious duplicants have a new stat to manage; "Gill Moisture", this can be increased by simply swimming in water or doing water based recreational activities. Dry gills will increasingly stress out the duplicant.
[*] alternatively to doing minnows quest, you can also roll her in the starting screen by selecting the "amphibious duplicant" in the model dropdown.
[*] Plastic beds have been made immune to flooding so minnow can sleep in her underwater clam bed.
[*] DuperyFixed Model Id: "AquaticMinion"
[/list]
