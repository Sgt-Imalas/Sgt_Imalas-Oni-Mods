[h1]Pip Planting Notification[/h1]

This mod adds a the option to Pips to be notified when they plant a seed.

[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Sgt-Imalas/Sgt_Imalas-Oni-Mods/refs/heads/master/Compat_All.png[/img]

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Pips now have a checkbox "notify on planting", disabled by default
[*] enabling the checkbox will create clickable notifications when a seed is planted by that pip.
[*] clicking the notification will highlight the cell the pip has planted in
[/list]
