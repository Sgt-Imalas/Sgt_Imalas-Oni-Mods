Ronivan's Legacy 1.10

New:

- high pressure pipes and rails now can alternatively be built from Tungsten
- added regolith as alternative ingredient in concrete recipe
- added bioplastic as an alternative ingredient for plasteel
- added permendur and plasteel as alternative ingredients for steel in drillbit recipes
- added silver ore to starting metal list

Changes & Tweaks:

- adjusted sulphuric acid synthesizer recipe to be physically accurate, added alternative recipe with pyrite
- flocculation sieve now takes any filtration medium instead of only sand
- sour water stripper now takes any filtration medium instead of only sand
- increased salt and bleachstone yield of chloroschist crushing
- removed co2 output from plasma furnace
- increased CNC machine material costs
- improved coral vat conversion rates to be more useful
- tweaked fridge pod costs to be in line with other fridges

Fixes:
- fixed plasma furnace not emitting liquid glass
- improved scaling of material selector height increases on buildings with lots of variants
- fixed zinc ore crashing metal hatches due to a missing element parameter
- fixed some minor typos
